The Michigan Daily is the campus newspaper of The University of Michigan; the paper is financially independent from the University, striving for journalistic integrity on a campus without a journalism school. New copies of The Daily are printed weekly and distributed across Ann Arbor, while the paper's website updates with new stories every day.
